## How This Wiki Was Built

This wiki was produced by **recursive sub-agent delegation** across two waves:

**Wave 1** (10 pages): A coordinator sub-agent spawned 8 parallel children, each reading source files and writing markdown pages. Two children (agent-system, whaleflow) received `max_depth=2` and spawned their own sub-sub-agents for deep-dive investigation.

**Wave 2** (4 pages): Four parallel children documented the skills system, fleet control plane, 29 supplementary tools, and internal runtime systems.

**Total: ~17 sub-agents across 3 depth levels.**
